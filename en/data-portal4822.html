Data portal

The Data portal contains all lists of enrolments, authorisations and registrations maintained by the FSMA. It also contains all documents regarding issues of securities and takeover bids, as well as information on listed companies and managers' transactions. You can find this information by clicking on the different sections or by using the search function.
